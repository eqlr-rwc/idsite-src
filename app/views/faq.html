I'm an existing customer visiting the new login page for the first time. Why must I reset my password?
We increased our security and moved our user identity /authentication to the cloud. Since all passwords are encrypted and unknown to us, we could not transport the passwords with the rest of the account data. By resetting your password you will be creating a password for the first time within our new and improved security framework.


How do I reset my password?
To reset your password please follow the steps below:

  • From the log in page click on the text 'Forgot Password?' underneath the sign in button.
  • A box should appear labeled 'Email'. Type the email address associated with your account into this box and click SEND.
  • Check the mail inbox for the email address you entered above. You should have received an email from Equilar. containing instructions to click on a reset password link. Click on this link or copy/paste the link into your browser's address bar to open up a dedicated reset password page.
  • Once on the reset password page, type a new password into the first input box and confirm the password by typing it again into the second input box. Click SUBMIT once done.
  • Congratulations, you have now reset your password! Return to the login page and you should be able to log in using your email address and the new password you have just created.


I see a message about enterprise.stormpath.io. What is Stormpath?
Stormpath is the user identity service that we use for authentication during the log in process. You can read about their security practices here: https://stormpath.com/product/password_security.


What browsers are supported?
We recommend Chrome for the best experience although Firefox and Internet Explorer version 10 and above are also supported. If you are trying to access Insight and are using another browser or an older browser version that may not be supported, you can also try logging in using our alternative login page here: https://insight.equilar.com/app/login/login2.jsp.


If I have more questions, who can I contact?
Please reach out to support@equilar.com. We will be happy to assist you with any questions you may have.
